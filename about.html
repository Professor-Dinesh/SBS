About AutoCare

Twelve years of keeping vehicles running safer, smoother, and longer — one honest service at a time.

Who We Are

A Full-Service Garage, Now Online

AutoCare began in 2014 as a single neighbourhood garage in Chennai with a simple promise: honest work, fair prices, and no waiting around. Today, we've grown into a network of certified service centres, backed by a simple online booking system that lets you reserve a slot from your phone.

Every technician on our floor is factory-trained and background-verified. We service hatchbacks, sedans, SUVs, and two-wheelers, using genuine parts and manufacturer-approved fluids, so your warranty stays intact.

Our booking platform was built to remove the guesswork from vehicle servicing — see prices upfront, pick a convenient slot, and track your appointment, all without a single phone call.
